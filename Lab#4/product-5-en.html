Силуети

SadSvit & СТРУКТУРА ЩАСТЯ

1000 ₴

Listen:

Immerse yourself in the atmosphere of Ukrainian post-punk. "Силуети" (Silhouettes) is a joint melancholic hit from SadSvit and СТРУКТУРА ЩАСТЯ (STRUKTURA SHCHASTIA) that became a soundtrack for many. The cold synthesizer sound, deep vocals, and signature "doomer" aesthetic create a unique mood that vinyl only emphasizes, adding volume to the sound. This single is a perfect addition to a collection of the modern Ukrainian "new wave".
